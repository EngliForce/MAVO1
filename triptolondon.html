🧳 The Situation

You will plan a weekend trip to London... all by yourself! From travel to food, from hotel to activities — you will plan everything with a real budget.

You will travel to London with a friend:

  • 📅 Arrival: Friday evening
  • 📅 Return: Monday morning
  • ⏳ Time: 3 nights
  • ❗ You have 2 full days in London.

💸 Budget

You get a budget of £425 per person. This budget must pay for:

  • ✈️ Travel (Friday and Monday)
  • 🏨 Hotel (3 nights)
  • 🎟️ Activities / Places to visit
  • 💰 + Pocket money
👉 Food & drinks: You get a separate budget of £60 per person for the weekend. Breakfast is included with the hotel, so you only pay for lunch and dinner on Saturday and Sunday.

📝 Your Task – What do you need to do?

1. ✈️ Travel

  • How do you travel to London? (plane, train, bus)
  • Times and prices
  • Why do you choose this?

2. 🏨 Hotel

  • Where do you sleep? (Make sure breakfast is included!)
  • Price per night
  • Description + review (for your final product)

3. 📅 Daily Schedule (for 2 full days)

Plan at least 3 activities per day (morning, afternoon, and evening). Think of: museums, shopping, parks, or shows.

4. 🍽️ Food & drinks

Lunch and dinner for EVERY day. Where do you eat? Prices must be real and stay under your weekend budget of £60.
