Have Got - present simple - bevestigend

Vul de juiste vorm van het werkwoord 'have got' in: have got of has got.

Gebruik de complete vorm, dus geen afkortingen.

1. I a new bike.
2. Sarah a cat.
3. They three children.
4. John and I a big house.
5. He a red car.
6. You a problem.
7. Emma a cold.
8. We many friends.
9. My parents a holiday home.
10. The dog a black spot.
