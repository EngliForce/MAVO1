📌 Instructions

This is maybe the most important thing to learn in a new language: introducing yourself! In Unit 1, we learn exactly how to do this.

Step 1: Write about yourself. Add a nice photo!
Step 2: Interview 4 classmates and write short stories about them.
Step 3: Save your cards and hand them in to your teacher.

What can you write about?

  • Your name, age, hometown, clothes, hobbies, and sports.
  • Your family, pets, fun facts, talents, and your dream job!
  • Be creative! You can always add other fun things about yourself.
